The Golden Plains


Welcome to the Golden Plains Stories! The shire is located north from metropolitan Melbourne in between Ballarat and geelong. It has a population over 24000 people the Golden Plains Shire is a local government area consisting of 16 townships and 56 communities with its main economic industries specialising in agriculture, construction, and wine manufacturing. Discover the shires rich history through our web museum!
